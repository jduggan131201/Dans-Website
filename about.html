You Buy, We Deliver...
for FREE

At PMG Motors, our expert team is dedicated to making your van buying experience simple, transparent, and completely hassle-free. Over the years, we’ve built a strong reputation and a loyal customer base, with many buyers returning to us time and time again. We believe this is because we focus on quality above all else — carefully sourcing only the best used vehicles on the market and preparing them to the highest possible standards, both cosmetically and mechanically.

Every vehicle we sell undergoes a rigorous 100-point safety inspection, giving you confidence that your van will perform reliably day in, day out — because we understand how important it is for your business to stay on the road 365 days a year.

Buying from us is designed to be flexible and convenient. You can purchase in person or over the phone, and we’re happy to send detailed walk-around videos straight to your smartphone. To ensure you’re always getting a fair deal, our prices are checked daily. Finance options are also quick and straightforward to arrange — simply speak to a member of our sales team to check your eligibility and complete an application.

To make things even easier, we offer FREE delivery in the North West on all vehicles, a service we’ve refined to be fast, reliable, and stress-free. From your first enquiry through to the moment your new van arrives, PMG Motors is here to provide a professional, trustworthy, and customer-focused experience every step of the way.
